# Chattanooga Generative AI Working Group

This repository is the working archive for AI Night decks, diagrams, and presentation artifacts created for the Chattanooga Generative AI Working Group.

The group is for people trying to keep up with fast-moving AI changes from a practical, technical angle. The focus is less on hype and more on what builders, developers, product people, and curious practitioners can actually learn, test, and build together.

The group is open to anyone curious about generative AI, but the center of gravity is technical: product, programming, tooling, experimentation, and honest discussion about what is worth building.

If that sounds like your kind of room, join the group on Meetup:

- [Chattanooga Generative AI Working Group on Meetup](https://www.meetup.com/chattanooga-generative-ai-working-group/)

## What To Expect

- Bi-weekly discussion and working sessions around current AI developments
- A technical, product-and-programming-oriented perspective on new models, tools, and workflows
- Conversation that ranges from hands-on implementation details to broader questions about where AI is useful, fragile, or overhyped
- A place to bring ideas, experiments, and half-formed projects and get sharper feedback

## What This Repository Holds

- Slide decks and visual materials used in meetup sessions
- Mermaid diagrams and supporting presentation assets
- Published HTML and PDF outputs for sharing after a session
